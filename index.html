Campus Witnessing

The mission field of the ASA is the campus. Here in the campus, ASA visit students during branch Sabbath School to share the gospel of Jesus Christ. Car park outreach is also major witnessing activity.

Bible Study

Bible study is the main activity of ASA. It is the objective of ASA that the members are not just actively studying their degree programs but also deligently studying the Holy scriptures to be Bible students.

Social Activity

ASA activities include car wash, mess cleaning, sports and milo nights. These are activities implemented in keep the ASA members physically fit and socially active.
